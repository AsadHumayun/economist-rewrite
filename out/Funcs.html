(async) getDragonAlias(uid) → {Array.<string, Array.<string>>}

This function will get the display name and the emojis for a user"s dragon alias. * Each user can have their own dragon alias (of course, I would have to add it to `petaliases.json` for it to be registered as such). * An "alias" allows a user to replace the "dragon" for anything else, as well as allowing them to choose custom emojis for their stats on their dragon. In the latter parts of the bot, there is a system that will allow users to give/take access of the alias from people. * Users are able to do `~dragonalias` to see a list of their aliases, indexed. They will then do `~dragonalias `, replacing `` with the index of their choice, which means that the bot will switch their alias to whatever they had chosen.
